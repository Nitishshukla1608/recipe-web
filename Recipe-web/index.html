Gulab Jamun..

Ingredients

  • Seema Powder
  • Sugar
  • Oil

Recipe

  1. Combine sugar and water in a pan and bring to a boil to create the syrup. Remove from heat, add cardamom, and set aside to cool.
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine flour, paneer, sooji, Nestlé MILKMAID, baking powder, and baking soda. Mix gently to form a soft dough, avoiding over-kneading.
  3. Divide the dough into 30-35 portions and gently shape them into round balls.
  4. Heat oil on low flame and fry the balls until they turn golden brown.
  5. Transfer the fried Gulab Jamuns into the cooled sugar syrup. Once all the Gulab Jamuns are added, bring the syrup to a boil again briefly, then remove from heat.
  6. Enjoy your homemade Gulab Jamun warm, garnished with your favourite toppings.

Kaaju Katli..

Ingredients

  • Cashews
  • Sugar
  • Oil

Recipe

  1. Grind Cashews – Blend cashews into a fine powder (don’t over-grind to avoid releasing oil).
  2. Prepare Sugar Syrup – In a pan, heat sugar and water over low flame until it forms a one-string consistency.
  3. Mix & Cook – Add the cashew powder to the syrup, stirring continuously until it thickens into a dough-like consistency.
  4. Knead & Roll – Transfer the mixture onto a greased surface, let it cool slightly, then knead it gently and roll it evenly.
  5. Cut & Serve – Cut into diamond shapes, apply silver vark (optional), and enjoy your homemade Kaaju Katli!

Laddu..

Ingredients

  • Boondi
  • Sugar
  • Food color

Recipe

  1. Roast Besan – In a pan, heat ghee and roast besan on low flame until golden brown and aromatic.
  2. Prepare Sugar – Grind sugar into a fine powder and mix it with cardamom powder.
  3. Mix Ingredients – Add the sugar mixture and nuts to the roasted besan, mix well, and let it cool slightly
  5. Shape Laddus – While warm, take small portions and roll them into round balls using your palms.
  6. Set & Serve – Let the laddus set for 15 minutes and enjoy!
